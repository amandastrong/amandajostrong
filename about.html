Let me introduce myself. My name is Amanda Strong and I am currently a graduate student at Northwestern University’s School of Communication.

I am a problem solver who has lived their life thinking outside of the box, using my unique background and talents to find creative and innovative solutions to problems both big and small. I have studied everything from engineering to communications and public relations, giving myself a unique perspective on the world around me. I love a good challenge, and have always found myself torn between my passion for both the arts and science.

While pursuing my MS in Leadership in Creative Enterprises at Northwestern University, I have learned how to merge my left brain with my right, putting both my technical knowledge and business savvy to use in creative environments.
